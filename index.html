About us

The quest for knowledge unites enthusiasts beyond all boundaries . Anvesha is the embodiment of that very spirit. IISER-TVM is still a relatively nascent addition to the indian academic scene. Constituted in 2009, the club took life as a platform for students to gather and share ideas beyond academics. Aptly named Anvesha to signify the raging curiosity and thirst for knowledge, the club has spread its wings and gained official recognition over the years. Since 2012, the club has conducted the annual science fest, with events spread through September and October, culminating in a three day extravaganza; a litmus test for all shades of intellectual prowess. The club is also continually committed to giving back to the society, through its outreach initiatives and awareness campaigns. We endeavour to bring the joy of embracing the spirit of curiosity to the masses.
